About Us

Viventum International Trading

Established in 2011, Viventum International Trading has grown into a trusted name in the healthcare and consumer products sector in Qatar. Since 2013, when a consortium from Muscat represented by Mr. D.S. Mehta (Ibn Sina Pharmacy, Oman) took stakes in Viventum, the company has been backed by over 40 years of pharmaceutical distribution expertise from Ibn Sina Pharmacy.

In 2015, we partnered with Pfizer to market select evolved products in Qatar, further strengthening our portfolio. Today, Viventum proudly represents renowned international companies, catering to more than 180 pharmacies as well as major hypermarkets and supermarkets across Qatar.

Our dedicated regulatory department, managed by a pharmacist with 17+ years of GCC experience, ensures smooth approvals and compliance. We are also active suppliers to Hamad Medical Corporation, managing both local and SGH tender supplies — a segment that has seen remarkable growth since 2017.

With a vibrant sales team, SCH-approved storage facilities, a robust delivery network, and a modern Oracle-based ERP system, we ensure efficient supply chain management and exceptional customer satisfaction.
